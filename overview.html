Overview

The process of creating a multiplayer game heavily relies on what is known as replication.

When you play multiplayer games with your friends, it may seem like things just seamlessly happen and you can just be thrown into a world with your friend and everything works, however games are specifically programmed to replicate specific events.

For example, if you were to program a game for singleplayer and then throw multiple people into the same server, you will notice that many things won't work properly, or even at all.

In order to make stuff actually sync between the players, you will need to learn how to replicate events between players to keep everything in sync.
